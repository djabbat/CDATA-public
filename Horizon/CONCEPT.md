## Core Hypothesis

Centrosome–Cytoskeleton Dynamics and Ageing (CDATA) theory posits that age-related cellular dysfunction is primarily driven by progressive dysregulation of centrosomal machinery, measurable through CAII activity as a proxy biomarker, and reversible through targeted cellular interventions.

**Novel biomarker:** Carbonic Anhydrase II (CAII) activity as a proxy for centrosome integrity and cellular age state.

- Method: U-ExM (ultra-expansion microscopy), Miltenyi MACS EPC isolation, Roche Cobas ELISA
